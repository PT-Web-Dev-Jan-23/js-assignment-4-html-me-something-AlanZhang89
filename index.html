My introduction

My name is Zhengwu Zhang. I'm from China.I've done a lot of
jobs so far, like I worked as an animator in China. Then came
to the United States to work as a Japanese food chef, delivery,
and now is working as a caricature.

Animator in China

"I learned to draw when I was young. My high school was an art school. So I chose animation design as my major in college. Because I had a dream when I was little to make an animation that could be shown on TV. My dream has come true. About three of the animations I worked on have been shown around the world. The title of the animation is The Configurators. This animation is exported abroad. Some cartoons are only shown in China."

caricature artist in The US

"I was looking for resumes and suddenly there was a company looking for a cartoonist. So I was particularly interested and sent in my resume. Luckily I got the job. I love this business. I see a lot of great people every day. They are all very enthusiastic. The love and surprise on their faces whenever I show them my work. Can make me very happy. I have been working in this field for more than 4 years."
